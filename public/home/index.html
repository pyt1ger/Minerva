Computer Science

Being a tech school, computer science is heavily stressed on Troy's campus, with a mandatory APCSP class within the TT program. Many students are inclined to take this pathway, but some struggle with the complex concepts and syntax.

Biology

Biology is also a heavily stressed class, as being a mandatory science course one usually takes during freshman year. In addition to this, AP & IB Bio can be challenging with it's countless concepts.

Chemistry

Chemistry is vastly different and yet correlated to other science subjects such as physics and biology.
